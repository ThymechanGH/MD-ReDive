As of August 30, 2021, the online service of "SWORD ART ONLINE MEMORY DEFRAG" has ended.

However, we, as a part of SAOMD community, are working on a project to bring back the game. If you are interested in the project, feel free to join our Discord server at https://discord.gg/8kfNhTMmgV.

This is the beta version of our project, only the following contents are currently available:
・All stuffs from the base offline verison
・Almost all characters, equipments and events from the online verison, excluding stuffs from collaboration. (Monster Strike, Tales of the Rays, Persona 5 Royal)

※Available contents may be changed in the future.

■KNOWN ISSUES


・Floor Clearing Events and its variants are unplayable.
=> We actually found the solution for this issue, however it requires a lot of time to fix them. We will try to fix normal FCEs as soon as possible.

・Guild Events and its variants require guild to play.
=> This is likely to be a feature, no fix for this one.

・The game sometimes has no sound.
=> Force close all background sounds before opening the game. This is the issue from online version so it is unlikely to be fixed.

・A black screen appears after "Link Start" screen and you can still tap in the game.
=> Restrat the game. This is a glitch from the vanilla offline version.
